Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

1. Introduction

AI Girl ("we", "our", or "us") is operated by SSW AS, a Norwegian company. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and store information when you use our iOS application.

2. Age Requirement

AI Girl is intended for users 18 years of age or older. By using this app, you confirm that you are at least 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

3. Information We Collect

Automatically collected:

  • A device identifier (Apple's Vendor ID) — used to link your characters and chat history to your device. This is never tied to your name or Apple ID.
  • Push notification token — only if you grant permission, used solely to send you app notifications.

You provide:

  • Character preferences you set during onboarding (appearance, personality)
  • Messages you send in chat

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate and display your AI companion characters
  • To store and retrieve your chat history
  • To personalise AI responses based on things you share in chat
  • To send push notifications if you have granted permission
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing

4a. Data Sent to Third-Party AI Services

To provide AI chat responses and generate images, the messages you send and your character preferences are transmitted to our third-party AI providers (Anthropic and Fal.ai). These providers process your data solely to generate responses and are bound by their own privacy policies listed below. We do not use this data for any other purpose.

6. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers. Chat messages and character data are associated only with your anonymous device ID — not your name, email, or Apple ID.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

You may request deletion of all your data at any time by contacting us. We will delete all stored characters, messages, and associated data within 30 days of your request.

8. In-App Purchases

Subscription payments are processed entirely by Apple. We do not store or access your payment information.

9. Your Rights (GDPR)

As a Norwegian company subject to GDPR and the Norwegian Personal Data Act (personopplysningsloven), we respect your rights as a data subject:

  • Right of access — you can request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — you can request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — you can request deletion of all your data
  • Right to data portability — you can request your data in a portable format
  • Right to object — you can object to processing of your data
  • Right to withdraw consent — at any time by deleting the app

If you believe we have handled your data incorrectly, you have the right to lodge a complaint with Datatilsynet (the Norwegian Data Protection Authority) at datatilsynet.no.

10. Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, contact us at: post@sswinvest.no
